Soundness improves your productivity and creativity by playing ambient sounds like those found in a coffeeshop or library. Research shows that a moderate level of ambient noise enhances creativity, while low levels boost productivity and information processing. Soundness is a neat little app that lives in your menubar that lets you toggle ambient sounds from places like coffeeshops, libraries, and parks.
